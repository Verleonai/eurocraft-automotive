Who We Are

European cars deserve European discipline.

EuroCraft Automotive was founded on a simple idea: the dealership doesn't have a monopoly on doing it right. Cars built with German, British, and Italian precision deserve service performed with the same standard — not a one-size-fits-all chain shop, and not a dealership that's spread too thin to actually focus on your car.

We built this shop around three things: manufacturer-level capability, transparent pricing, and honest communication. No upsells. No "while we have it open" surprises. Just precise work performed by technicians who have spent careers under European hoods.

What We Stand For

Three values, every job.

These aren't slogans — they're the rules that decide how we treat every vehicle and every owner.

Precision

Every torque spec respected. Every fluid measured. Every code logged and post-scan verified. Precision isn't optional — it's the entire job.

Transparency

You get the diagnostic, the quote, the parts list, and the labor breakdown — before we lift a tool. No hidden line items. No theater.

Capability

We invest in the same tools, scanners, and programming systems the dealership uses — because European vehicles demand it, and shortcuts cause callbacks.
