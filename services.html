Our Capabilities

Precision work,
without the dealership.

Eight core disciplines performed with factory-level diagnostic systems, OEM-spec parts, and the discipline your European vehicle was engineered for.

Eight Core Disciplines

Every system. Every model.

From routine factory maintenance to deep ECU calibration — these are the eight disciplines we perform every week, on every European platform we service.

01 · Maintenance
01

Scheduled Maintenance

Factory-scheduled service using OEM or equivalent-quality parts, performed to manufacturer specifications. Done correctly, scheduled service at EuroCraft does not affect your factory warranty — and it costs far less than the dealer.

  • OEM or equivalent-spec parts and fluids
  • Manufacturer service interval reset
  • Multi-point inspection on every visit
  • Digital service record kept on file
02 · Diagnostics
02

Advanced Diagnostics

Manufacturer-level scan tools and pinpoint diagnostic systems for accurate fault isolation. We don't throw parts at problems — we identify the root cause and quote it transparently.

  • Diesel engine service and aftertreatment
  • DEF / AdBlue system diagnostics
  • Emissions, EVAP, and fuel system work
  • Mercedes Sprinter & commercial vehicles
  • Full network scan: engine, trans, body, chassis
03 · Engine
03

Engine Repair

From minor issues to complete rebuilds — focused on reliability, performance, and long-term durability. We service forced-induction platforms, V8s, V10s, and inline-sixes from every major European OEM.

  • Timing chain, guide, and tensioner service
  • Head gasket, valve cover, and oil leak repair
  • Turbocharger and supercharger work
  • Cooling system and water pump overhauls
  • Engine assembly and partial rebuilds
04 · Brakes
04

Brake Systems

Complete brake service — pads, rotors, fluid flushes, calipers, and brake-by-wire diagnostics. We work on OEM, ceramic, and performance big-brake-kit setups for street and track.

  • OEM and performance pad & rotor packages
  • Full brake fluid flush and bleed
  • Caliper rebuild and replacement
  • Big brake kit (BBK) installation
  • PCCB / carbon-ceramic system service
05 · Suspension
05

Suspension

Full inspection and service of every suspension component. Whether you want to restore factory ride quality or sharpen the handling on a performance build, we'll get the geometry right.

  • Air suspension diagnosis and rebuild
  • Coilover and lift installation
  • Control arm, bushing, and link replacement
  • Shock and strut service
  • Performance ride-height calibration

06 · Alignment
06

Wheel Alignment

Precision alignment for proper handling, tire life, and stability — including advanced setups for lowered cars, performance builds, and specialty vehicles where standard rack specs don't apply.

  • OEM-spec four-wheel alignment
  • Performance camber and toe setups
  • Track-spec alignments with corner balancing
  • Steering angle sensor calibration
07 · Programming
07

Programming & Coding

Software updates, control module programming, and system coding using factory-level systems (ISTA, ODIS, XENTRY, PIWIS) — work most independents simply can't perform.

  • Feature coding (lighting, comfort, retrofits)
  • Control module replacement and adaptation
  • Software updates and recall completion
  • Module-level repair and reprogramming
  • Vehicle order and PIA / FA coding
08 · Performance
08

Performance / Tuning

ECU and TCU calibration designed for safe, reliable power gains — paired with the supporting hardware your platform actually needs to live with it long-term.

  • Stage 1 / 2 / 3 ECU calibration
  • DCT / DSG / PDK transmission tuning
  • Custom tuning for built engines
  • Logging, dyno verification, and refinement
  • Supporting hardware (intake, exhaust, hardware)
